Tasmania

Tasmania is the outcasted state of Australia and we won't stand for it any longer. We have had companies like Woolsworths and Coles be able to corner a market due to people not believing there is a market here. More and more we are pushed apart by a tide of rhetoric, without even being land tied. If a country like Estonia can force independence while next to countries, surely a sea surrounded island can. We don’t need Australians, or Australia; in fact “Mainlanders” have caused higher housing rates and prices to increase from tourism. I don’t hate tourism, but 5$ for a can is outrageous, and that’s just the beginning.

We barely need australia. We make enough from exports to support ourselves where Australia just takes our homes and causes people to not be able to afford homes. Why should a basic 3 bedroom house cost more than half a million? It’s a travesty, and it’s been caused partly by the mainlanders, with landlords taking advantage.
